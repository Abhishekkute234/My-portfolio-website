I'm a passionate developer with a strong focus on **Generative AI**, **Data Science**, and **Full-Stack Development**. I love bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI models and beautiful, user-centric web applications.

Here is a deep dive into my expertise and the technologies I work with every day.

---

## 🤖 Generative AI & Machine Learning

The AI landscape is evolving rapidly, and I am actively building solutions that leverage state-of-the-art models. My focus is on creating intelligent, context-aware applications.

- **Large Language Models (LLMs)**: OpenAI GPT, Llama, Hugging Face, Gemini.
- **RAG & Vector Databases**: Pinecone, ChromaDB, LangChain, LlamaIndex for Retrieval-Augmented Generation.
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting optimized prompts for structured and precise AI outputs.
- **Deep Learning**: Building and fine-tuning models using TensorFlow and PyTorch.

---

## 📊 Data Science & Analytics

Data is the new oil, and I specialize in extracting actionable insights from raw data. Whether it's training predictive models or building interactive dashboards, I cover the entire data lifecycle.

### Data Science
- **Machine Learning**: Scikit-learn, XGBoost for classification, regression, and clustering.
- **Data Wrangling**: Cleaning and transforming complex datasets using **Pandas** and **NumPy**.
- **Model Evaluation**: Ensuring high accuracy and robustness in predictive modeling.

### Data Analyst
- **Data Visualization**: Transforming numbers into stories using Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.
- **Statistical Analysis**: Descriptive statistics, A/B testing, and hypothesis testing to drive business decisions.
- **Database Querying**: Writing complex, optimized **SQL** queries to extract insights.

---

## 💻 Full-Stack Development

I build scalable, modern web applications from the database up to the user interface, with a strong emphasis on performance and aesthetics.

### Next.js & React
- **Next.js**: My framework of choice. I leverage the App Router, Server Components, and Server Actions for blazing-fast, SEO-optimized web apps.
- **React**: Building highly interactive and rich UI components.
- **Styling**: **Tailwind CSS** for rapid, responsive design, and **Framer Motion** for buttery-smooth animations.

### Backend & Infrastructure
- **Node.js & Express**: Building robust RESTful and GraphQL APIs.
- **Databases**: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, and Supabase.
- **ORMs**: Prisma and Drizzle for type-safe database interactions.
- **Cloud & Deployment**: Vercel, AWS, and Docker.
